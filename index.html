When planning their “holiday of a lifetime” one cold winter’s evening in Carbis Bay, the Bedboroughs and Veals made the brave decision go sailing together on a yacht in Greece for summer 2022. Our pre-requisite list included having someone on board who knew the area well and could take us into little bays and inlets for snorkelling and swimming. As there were 8 of us we also needed a boat big enough to give us all a little space. After much searching and consideration of the various options we opted for a week on a skippered 50 foot sailing yacht “Big Mama “ exploring the Lefada and the surrounding islands in the Ionian Sea. We agreed to follow this with one week onshore in an Air BnB where we could chill out by the pool and the kids could re-associate themselves with WiFi and their phones.
