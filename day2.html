Left Vlicho - Motor Sail past Megnaisis – sails up – tacking with Tegen steering.

Then to Ithaca - anchored in the bay then we went swimming. Lunchtime beach -Tomasso cooked up some fantastic pesta, feta and tomato pasta .

We then motored to the next bay around called Filiastro which was a bit busier and anchored alongside his friend Angel(in)o who was also on charter.

Keith did an Open Water buoy and we messed around on the SUP. In the evening Tomoaso took us in the tender to the nearby Tavern when the Veals enjoyed some Mojitos and we had a fantastic meal. We then took the tender back under the stars to Big Mama.
